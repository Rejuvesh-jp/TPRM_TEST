Workflow

Upload the vendor's completed security questionnaire. Our parser extracts every question, response, and section automatically.

Add Security Artifacts
Upload SOC reports, ISO certificates, penetration tests, and other evidence. AI chunks, embeds, and indexes each document for retrieval.

AI-Powered Gap Analysis
